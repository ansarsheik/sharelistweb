Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I signup for an account?
    Tap the Sign up button and Continue with Google account or Enter your details to signup.
  • How do I create a new list?
    Tap the Add List button, enter a name for your list, and hit save.
  • How do I add tasks to a list?
    Open your list, then tap the Add Task button. Enter the task details and save it.
  • How do I mark tasks as important or completed?
    Tap any task to mark it as important or complete. You can toggle these states anytime.
  • Can I share lists with others?
    Yes! Tap the share icon on any list and choose a options you want to share the list.
